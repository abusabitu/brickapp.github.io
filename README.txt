Brick App Landing Page
Overview

The Brick App landing page introduces users to Brick, a mobile app that lets people support retail outlets by backing the supplies they need. The site is designed to clearly communicate app benefits and encourage downloads.

The design is modern, clean, and responsive, using black, white, and grey with subtle red accents for emphasis. 

Key Features

Hero section with tagline and call-to-action

App screenshots showcasing functionality and user experience

Feature highlights with icons

Download badges for Google Play and App Store (placeholders included)

Sticky download bar for easy access

Footer with contact info, address, and social links

Fully responsive and mobile-friendly


Branding

Colors: Black, white, grey, subtle red

Typography: Clear, modern fonts

Focus: Encourage users to download the app and support retailers
