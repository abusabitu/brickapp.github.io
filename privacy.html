We collect data to provide, maintain, and improve our services, facilitate transactions, and ensure the security of our platform.

This includes information you actively submit to us:

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected data for the following essential business and service-related purposes:

  • **Service Provision:** To authenticate your account, allow you to view offers, back supplies, and manage your profile.
  • **Transaction Processing:** To facilitate secure payments and track all funds related to supply backing.
  • **Communication:** To send you service updates, security alerts, and transaction receipts.
  • **Security and Fraud Prevention:** To enforce our Terms of Service and detect, prevent, and respond to illegal activities or technical issues.
  • **Service Improvement:** To monitor, analyze, and optimize the functionality and user experience of the App.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We share information only as necessary to run our business, provide the service, and comply with the law. We **do not sell your personal data**.

  • **With Service Providers:** We share data with vendors, consultants, and service providers who need access to perform services on our behalf (e.g., Payment Processors, Cloud Hosting).
  • **With Partnering Businesses:** When you back a supply request, we share necessary non-financial profile information (e.g., username) with the relevant store/business to enable tracking and fulfillment.
  • **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order).
  • **Business Transfers:** In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition.

4. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

  • **Data Retention:** We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Policy, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
  • **Account Deletion:** Upon verified request (submitted via our dedicated deletion form), we will initiate the permanent deletion of your personal data from our active systems.
