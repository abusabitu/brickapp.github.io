If you see a security prompt from your browser, tap Settings and enable permission to install apps from this source.

4. Locate and install the APK

Once the download is complete, open your Downloads folder or swipe down from the top of your screen. Tap on the file to begin installation.

5. Open Brick

After installation, you can launch the app from your home screen or app drawer.
